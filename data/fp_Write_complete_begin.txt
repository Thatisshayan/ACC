Hey there! 👋 Welcome to the super simple guide for using **@OurAccbot** – your friendly AI assistant on Telegram. No tech skills needed. Let's go!

---

### 🤖 1. What is this bot?
Think of it as a smart friend you can text anytime. It can answer questions, write things, find info, and help with tasks – all inside Telegram. Just type or tap, and it replies like a human.

---

### 🚀 2. How to start
1. Open Telegram and search for **@OurAccbot**.
2. Tap **Start** (or type `/start`).
3. The bot will say hello and show you a menu with buttons. That's it – you're ready!

---

### 📋 3. The main menu buttons
- **💬 Chat** – Start a normal conversation. Ask anything.
- **📝 Tasks** – Tell the bot to do something for you (write, research, plan).
- **🌍 Translate** – Translate text into any language.
- **🔍 Search** – Find jobs, news, or info.
- **⚙️ Settings** – Change how the bot talks to you (short/long answers, language).
- **❓ Help** – Get this guide or contact support.

---

### 💡 4. 10 things you can ask (copy-paste these!)
1. "What should I cook with chicken and rice?"

9. "Summarize this article: [paste link]"
10. "What's the weather in London?"

---

### ⌨️ 5. Commands cheat sheet (10 most useful)
- `/start` – Begin or reset the bot
- `/help` – Show this guide
- `/short` – Make answers shorter
- `/long` – Make answers detailed
- `/translate [language] [text]` – Translate anything
- `/task [your task]` – Tell the bot to do a job
- `/search [query]` – Find info or jobs
- `/clear` – Delete our chat history
- `/stop` – Pause the bot (it will stop replying)
- `/settings` – Open settings menu

---

### ✂️ 6. Tips: How to get shorter answers
- Type `/short` before asking.
- Or say: "Keep it short" or "In 2 sentences."
- In Settings, choose "Short answers" mode.

---

### 🚫 7. What the bot CANNOT do (honest limitations)
- ❌ **Remember past chats** after you clear history.
- ❌ **Make phone calls** or send voice messages.
- ❌ **Access your personal data** (it's private).
- ❌ **Give medical/legal advice** – always ask a real doctor/lawyer.
- ❌ **Guarantee 100% accuracy** – sometimes it makes mistakes. Double-check important stuff.
- ❌ **Work offline** – needs internet.

---

### 🆘 8. How to get help
- Type `/help` anytime.
- Tap the **❓ Help** button in the menu.
- If something's broken, contact support: [support@ouraccbot.com](mailto:support@ouraccbot.com) or @OurAccSupport on Telegram.

---

That's it! You're now ready to chat with @OurAccbot. Try saying "Hi" or ask your first question. Have fun! 🎉
