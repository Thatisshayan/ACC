Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 30, 2026 · Applies to acccommand.center

ACC (Agent Command Center) is operated by Shayan Ezraeil, based in Canada. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how we protect it. We comply with Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) and Canada's Anti-Spam Legislation (CASL).

1. What We Collect

  • Account data: Email address, used to authenticate you via magic link (Supabase).
  • Waitlist data: Email, and optional qualification fields (what you want to automate, your role, your preference for control). Collected only when you submit the waitlist form.
  • Usage data: Task logs, agent outputs, and commands you issue through ACC. Stored to power the agent memory feature and to improve the product.
  • Payment data: Billing handled by Stripe. We do not store card numbers. Stripe's privacy policy applies to payment processing.
  • Voice input: If you use voice commands, audio is transcribed server-side via OpenAI Whisper and immediately discarded. We do not store audio recordings.

2. Why We Collect It

  • To authenticate you and provide access to your dashboard
  • To run AI agents on your behalf (the core product)
  • To send product updates and onboarding emails (only with your consent)
  • To process subscriptions and payments
  • To improve reliability and fix bugs

3. Who We Share It With

We do not sell your data. We share it only with service providers necessary to run ACC:

  • Supabase — authentication and database (servers in US/EU)
  • Stripe — payment processing
  • OpenAI — AI model inference and voice transcription
  • Resend — transactional email (magic links, receipts)
  • Railway — cloud hosting
  • Twilio — SMS and voice (if you use phone integrations)

All providers are bound by their own privacy policies and data processing agreements.

4. Data Retention

  • Account data: retained while your account is active, deleted within 30 days of account deletion request
  • Task logs and agent memory: retained for the lifetime of your account, exportable on request
  • Waitlist data: retained until you unsubscribe or request deletion
  • Voice audio: never stored — transcribed and discarded in real time

5. Your Rights (PIPEDA)

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Withdraw consent and request deletion
  • Unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email support@acccommand.center. We respond within 10 business days.

6. Email Communications

By joining the waitlist or creating an account, you consent to receive product updates and onboarding emails from ACC. Every email includes an unsubscribe link. You can also opt out by emailing support@acccommand.center with "Unsubscribe" in the subject line.

7. Security

We use HTTPS for all data in transit, Supabase row-level security for database access, and environment-variable-only storage for API keys (never in source code). Stripe handles all payment data in a PCI-compliant environment.

8. Cookies

ACC uses only functional cookies required for authentication (Supabase session token). We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies.

9. Children

ACC is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the product evolves. Material changes will be communicated by email. The "last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

Contact

Questions about your privacy? Contact us at support@acccommand.center or hello@acccommand.center.
