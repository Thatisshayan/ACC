// HOW IT WORKS

Three steps.
Then walk away.

01
You give one command
Type, speak, or send via Telegram. The intent parser understands plain English and routes your request to the right agent graph. No configuration needed.
02
Agents execute in parallel
ACC fans out across Claude, GPT-4o, Gemini and DeepSeek simultaneously. If one fails, a fallback takes over. You never notice. Results merge automatically.
03
You approve. It ships.
Before anything touches the outside world, a Telegram ping asks for your sign-off. One tap. Results are logged to Supabase, Notion and ClickUp automatically.
